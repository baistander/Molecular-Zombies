How to Play:

The object of the game is to save as many civilians (in green) from the zombies (in red).

Cure zombies by switching to a vaccination gun. Be careful though, civilians will mutate into shadow zombies if they've been infected several times. Shadow zombies are faster and hard to spot.

 

Controls:

Press the Up, Down, Left, and Right keys to control your character. Press spacebar to switch to your vaccination gun.

Accelerometer controls for mobile coming soon.
